Turn ATAC/ChIP-seq data into scientific insights

As a computational platform designed specifically for large-scale epigenomic studies, EAP is able to provide efficient and reproducible data analysis, which can lead to biologically meaningful findings and can broaden our understanding of epigenetic machinery in tissue development and disease progression.

Built for biologists
MAnorm2, HyperChIP, motifscanR and other analyzing tools (e.g., bowtie, MACS and HOMER) integrated into reproducible environments (docker images). Run locally or in the cloud.

Intuitive no-code UI

Preprocess raw sequencing data, perform differential analysis, and visualize epigenomic-defined subgroups or transcrpitional activity with a few clicks.

Scalable & secure

Deploy on laptop, HPC, or private cloud — keep your IP safe while scaling to large projects.

Comprehensive functionality

EAP has been designed to provide more analytical tools for functional downstream analyses, enabling users to delve deeper into the heterogeneity in their data as well as the associated transcriptional regulation.

Publication-ready output

Export figures and validated results suitable for manuscripts and reports.
